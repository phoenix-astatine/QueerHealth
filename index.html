Our Story and Mission

At Queer Health Club, we are committed to enhancing the health and well-being of LGBTQ+ individuals in West Africa. Our mission is to create an inclusive healthcare environment that ensures equitable access to quality care and wellness resources.

Founded in 2025, our initiative focuses on addressing unique challenges faced by marginalized communities and empowering individuals to lead healthier, happier lives. We work to foster a sense of belonging and strength through community support, education, and advocacy.
